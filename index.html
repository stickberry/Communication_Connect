About

COMMUNICATION : WHAT NEXT

Communication is the essence of existence. In all walks of life, one must be able to convey their ideas in ways that drive effective decision making, teamwork and action.

In this two-day workshop, the participants will gain a deeper understanding of elements of communication in domain of Information Technology, Applied Sciences, Management Studies and Electronics and Communication from the very best in their respective field. The workshop will surely make you more proficient tech enthusiast and better at articulating communication. The workshop will also help expand the horizon for the participants as they will be able to connect themselves with the prevailing practices in different domains of education.

Participants will also get to delve into the tools and techniques of communication which are presently trending and are purposeful. Exploring several communication types, this program will help in developing the written and oral communication skills in a range of settings. Through experimental exercises and small group activities, participants will put these techniques into practice, test out new approaches and learn to communicate with poise, clarity and conviction. You will leave the programme with the skills required to engage, inform and inspire others.
